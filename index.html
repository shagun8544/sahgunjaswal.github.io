About Us

Introduction

Each time a woman stands up for herself, without knowing its possibly, without claiming it, she stands up for all women. What is FEMINISM ? Simply the belief that a woman should as free as men, however nuts, dim deluded, badly dressed, receding, lazy, smug they might be. ARE YOU FEMINIST? Of course you are…..

Who am I…???

A long visionary & empowered girl, having an ideology of a world where financially and intellectually independent people with choices on one side, & on another, seeding the belief that a girl is equal to everyone else that we have the same rights thus can choose what and where to be and what to do in their lives.

How it all began…???

Being from a humble family, there were many challenges to be faced since birth. My parents are teachers by profession. So it was very common to have an environment full of family expectations in terms of studies and career choices. As a result, I was very focused on my studies and was a topper of my class. As I grew up, I used to take part in all the competitions including all the academic as well as those related to co-curricular activities. However, the willingness to win was the key to my winning attitude.Hence, the journey to my success began.

Beginning of a New Chapter…

Since the days of my higher education, I always dreamt of completing my studies abroad. So I worked hard to accomplish my dreams. During the days of my initial struggle, although working hard for certain years, MY FIRST CHALLENGE was to convince my family to take a serious decision regarding my future which was to, send me abroad for higher studies. However, it wasn’t possible for me to take a decision against them and they didn’t permitted to do so.

Opinion Matters…… for me it was MY PARENTS…

My parents opinion was that I must study properly focusing entirely on my education and career ahead. They didn’t wanted me to have a life full of struggle for my basic needs. AND finally, I had to drop my plan unwillingly thinking that it might be beneficial in one way or the other.

Taking a Turning point…..

As soon as I completed my graduation in Horticulture & Agriculture, I started looking for an option to survive on my own feet. Therefore, I started giving interviews from my college campus and got selected in various top listed firms of Tri-city. Notwithstanding, to opt a career under any pressurized situation along with a feeling stating that wont allow me to work superior to somebody including the zeal to own a business… All these things pushed me to build myself out of my comfort zone.

The day I joined my first Paid job

Before Joining the Office I was dedicated enough to work sincerely. Along with my efforts it was my fortune what made my name in my field. I joined Immigration as it was my own dream to migrate to another country for higher studies….. ANYHOW, I was unable to fulfill my ambition but didn’t wanted somebody else to sacrifice their Dreams for the sake of their parents fear and insecurity to build up the perfect CHOICE for them.

Self belief and Self realization

Often it’s the deepest pain which empowers you to grow into your highest self. This is your Power that you attract the vibes that are innate inside you. You are the most influential person, you will talk to whole day.
